# Business Potential

LabSync is positioned not only as a technical solution, but also as a commercial product with clear revenue and licensing models.

## Market Opportunity

Remote Monitoring and Management (RMM) is a mature market with demand from:

- education institutions with computer labs
- small and medium enterprises managing hybrid fleets
- IT service providers supporting distributed workstations
- research facilities and laboratories

Key benefits for customers:

- centralized control of both Windows and Linux devices
- one dashboard for monitoring, automation, and support
- lower operational overhead compared to multiple point tools
- ability to replace expensive proprietary RMM suites with a lean, extensible platform

## Value Proposition

LabSync delivers value through:

- unified management of mixed operating systems
- secure remote desktop and SSH access
- automated script execution and scheduling
- device grouping for bulk operations
- extensibility through module plugins

This creates a strong commercial proposition for companies that need to manage a large number of endpoints without introducing separate tools for each OS.

## Pricing Model

### Subscription-Based Licensing

The core business model is a per-machine subscription charged monthly. This model scales with the customer’s fleet and provides recurring revenue.

Suggested tiers:

- **Small Package:** 25 devices
- **Medium Package:** 100 devices
- **Large Package:** 250 devices
- **Enterprise Package:** 500+ devices

Each package includes:

- access to server and frontend
- agent deployment on licensed machines
- remote support and script automation
- standard updates for the license period

### Time-Limited License Options

Offer time-bound licenses for companies that want predictable costs:

- 1-year license
- 2-year license
- 3-year license

Longer license terms should include a discount for volume commitment and reduce churn by ensuring longer customer retention.

### Revenue Example

A practical pricing structure might be:

- 25 devices: monthly fee
- 100 devices: discounted monthly rate per device
- 250 devices: enterprise rate with support bundle
- 500+ devices: custom pricing with premium support

For example:

- 25 devices package: 25 x 10 EUR / month
- 100 devices package: 100 x 8 EUR / month
- 250 devices package: 250 x 6 EUR / month
- 500+ devices: negotiable enterprise price

This model creates clear growth potential from small teams to large organizations.

## Product Packages

### Single-Machine Subscription

Charge each managed device a monthly fee. This is simple and aligns with customer usage.

### Packaged Device Bundles

Offer packages for a fixed number of machines:

- **Starter Bundle:** up to 25 devices
- **Growth Bundle:** up to 100 devices
- **Professional Bundle:** up to 250 devices
- **Enterprise Bundle:** 500+ devices

Bundle benefits:

- easier procurement for customers
- predictable monthly billing
- simplified onboarding

### Annual and Multi-Year Bundles

Offer upfront payment options for 1, 2, or 3 years. Benefits include:

- stable cash flow
- stronger customer commitment
- predictable renewal cycles
- opportunity to offer premium support or training discounts

## Target Customers

### Education and Research

- computer laboratories at universities
- research clusters with shared lab workstations
- computer science classes requiring remote management

### Small and Medium Enterprises

- IT teams supporting mixed Windows/Linux environments
- managed service providers offering endpoint support
- companies with remote or distributed employees

### Larger Enterprises

- medium businesses with 500+ desktops
- organizations that require secure remote support
- companies seeking an affordable alternative to large RMM vendors

## Competitive Differentiators

LabSync stands out because of:

- true cross-platform support in one dashboard
- modular architecture for extensibility
- low-cost deployment using open-source and .NET technologies
- focus on laboratory and educational use cases
- live remote desktop plus SSH access in one product

## Summary

LabSync can be offered as a subscription service with device-based pricing, and time-limited licenses covering one, two, or three years. This approach is particularly attractive for institutions that manage many computers and want a predictable, scalable management solution.
